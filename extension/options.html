myRepairTools - Options

AI Assistant:

A ✨ button inside RepairQ that opens the CPR Assistant — Knowledge Base answers with citations, right on the ticket. Sign in to myRepairTools once on this browser and it just works.




RingCentral SMS:

Text customers straight from RepairQ. Hitting Ready for Pickup pops a "send text to Primary / Alt" chooser, pulled from the ticket's own numbers; the message goes out from the store line and is logged with your name.





What's Next? (order board):

A 🍔 button in RepairQ's top bar. One tap ranks the workable queue — express first, then overdue, then due-soonest — and tells the tech which ticket to grab. The 📺 board view shows the whole ranked queue.






RepairQ workflow tools:

Absorbed from the MyCPRTools extension — quality gates and shortcuts for the counter flow.










LCD Buyback:

Pops the Good/Bad display modal when a screen-repair line item is added for an enabled device family, and prints the send-display label with the ticket label. Logs to the MRT LCD Buyback Log.

  • 🏬 Texts now go out from your store's own number — Eugene, Salem and Clackamas each send from their own line (the ticket's store picks it automatically; stores without their line connected yet fall back to the Salem number).
Version 2.5.9

When you hit Ready for Pickup: text auto-sends to the saved number with a 5-second Undo — no chooser; call / email / return show a reminder of what the customer picked; and with nothing saved you still get the manual chooser.
